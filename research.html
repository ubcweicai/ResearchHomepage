Research Projects

Scalable Blockchain for Offline Payments over Bidirectional Channels

2018
funded by NSERC Engage Grant
The objectives are (1) to design bidirectional payment channels crossing multiple parties, and (2) to design an architecture for offline payment with these payment channesl.

Cognitive Platform for Ubiquitous Cloud-based Gaming

2012 - 2017
funded by NSERC Strategic Project Grant
Focus on integrating cloud resource to facilitate cloud gaming with capability of cognitive QoE-oriented resource optimization. For cloud gaming research, please refer to our paper "A Survey on Cloud Gaming: Future of Computer Games" published in IEEE ACCESS.

The People & Planet Friendly Home

2011 - 2014
Supported by UBC ICICS, NSERC, and a TELUS-led industrial consortium
The People & Planet Friendly Home is a large-scale, multidisciplinary research endeavour spearheaded by ICICS that seeks to develop a unique sustainable housing solution that is both environmentally friendly and improves the quality of life for the occupants of these spaces.
